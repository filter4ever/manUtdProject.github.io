Scott McTominay

Player's Bio
Position Midfielder
Country Scotland
Date Of Birth 8 Dec 1996

Scott McTominay also stands out to players in the pitch with his height of 193cm. Playing an all-round midfielder, he is willing to do anything to win. He will dribble, pass, shoot and defend. Previously playing as a more attacking midfielder in his days in the club youth academy, it is amazing how much passion he has for the club to the point he is willing to play in a position which he is unfamiliar with and definitely outside his comfort zone. He still has a lot to improve on though despite showing a hint of what he could do this season.
