Ashley Young

Player's Bio
Position Full Back
Country England
Date Of Birth 9 Jul 1985

Ashely Young joined the club on 1 July 2011 as a winger and made his debut on 7 August 2011 against Manchester City winning 3-2 and making an assist. He also scored two goals in the incredible 8-2 win over Arsenal outside the box. Basically, he came into the club with a huge bang and making a statement. He is still now in the club after 8 long years and despite not starting for a few seasons, now he is a regular as a full back on the left or the right and as a captian choice for a number of games this season.
