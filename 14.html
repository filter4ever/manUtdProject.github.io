Jesse Lingard

Player's Bio
Position Midfielder
Country England
Date Of Birth 15 Dec 1992

Also from the youth academy, Jesse Lingard is a proud Red Devil and has stayed with the club ever since. It took a while for him to get game time but eventually in 2015, he started to slowly gain game time and now he is one of the face of the club in terms of marketing with his memorable celebrations and online banter. Despite his small frame and height of 175cm, he takes care of himself pretty well by going to the gym regularly and not hesitating to show his body off. He is consistently working hard to run up and down the field to help with both attack and defence for the team with his unbelievable stamina. Not only that, his passing and shooting ability is not something to be taken lightly by the opposition with a respectable amount of goals as a midfielder under his belt.
