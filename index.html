A place for Manchester United Fans to prosper!

This is Manchester United, a football club with a long and prestigious history. Currently, the club has amassed 13 Premier League titles, 21 FA Community Shields and 3 Champions Leagues. Manchester United is also the first English club to win the European Cup in 1968, the Club World Cup in 2008 and the only English club to win the Intercontinental Cup in 1999. The club has also a huge number of legends under her belt such as Sir Bobby Charlton, George Best, Bobby Moore, etc.
