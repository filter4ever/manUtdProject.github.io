Anthony Martial

Player's Bio
Position Winger
Country France
Date Of Birth 5 Dec 1995

He has been dubbed the second Thierry Henry. Also a former Monaco man, he is Anthony Martial. A frenchman with amazing skills in his belt, he is undeniably very entertaining to watch. Joining the club at the age of 20, he made his debut against Liverpool, not a game to be taken lightly. That did not seem to affect the young frenchman though as in that game, he scored a goal which contributed the 3-1 win. Immediately after that game, he has always been one of the fan favourites in the club. Now a rotating winger with Alexis Sanchez, he hopes he can get more playing time in the future in support with the fans.
