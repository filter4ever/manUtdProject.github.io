Eric Bailly

Player's Bio
Position Centre Back
Country Ivory Coast
Date Of Birth 12 Dec 1994

Eric Bailly joined Manchester United on 8 June 2016. He made his debut for the club in a cup match against Leicester City and got Man of the Match. A week later, he made his league debut for the club against Bournemouth and got the Man of the Match as well. That month he was voted as Manchester United's August 2016 Player of the Month. This shows that he has the capability of being a world class centre back individually and also for the good of the club. A aggressive but composed force at the back, he could win the ball at the back and send the ball forward for a counter attack. However, being injury prone, he has been greatly missed at the back rather quickly and has been critizised for not being able to take care of himself for the good of the club. But when he is back on the field, fans and club members can see his determination everytime he plays.
