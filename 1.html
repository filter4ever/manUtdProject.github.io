David De Gea

Player's Bio
Position Goalkeeper
Country Spain
Date Of Birth 7 Nov 1990

David De Gea joined Manchester United on 2 July 2011 after being scouted personally by the one and only Sir Alex Ferguson, whch is fun fact, one of the only two days where he missed a game for Manchester United, the other day he skipped was for his son's wedding. Manchester United was also looking for a long term replacement for Edwin Van der Sar who was 41 at the time. So David had big shoes to fill if he were to join. This by of itself shows how much potential Sir Alex Ferguson saw in the then 21 year old. Fast forward to 8 years later, David de Gea is now one of the best if not the best goalkeeper in world football currently. Despite a shakey start, Sir Alex Ferguson got his back and lead him to greatness not just as an individual but also as a living club legend.
