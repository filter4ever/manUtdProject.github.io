Paul Pogba

Player's Bio
Position Midfielder
Country France
Date Of Birth 15 Mar 1993

A product of the club's youth academy, he represents what the youth academy is capable of producing. Now, he is one of the main players if not the main player the club is played around with. With his towering height of 191cm, he has a presence on the pitch that can only by rivaled by his constantly changing hair styles and colours. Not only does he has a physical dominance on the field but he also has dribbling skills, fans could only expect by small nimble players. His vision is also world class as he can pick out passes quicker than the oppositions could react. He is not shy from doing defensive duties as well. He could be seen on the field doing a bit of everything and when he gets going. He can be seen everywhere being a force to be reckon with. One thing is for sure, bringing him back from Juventus was worth it despite the huge financial loss.
