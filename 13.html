Lee Grant

Player's Bio
Position Goalkeeper
Country England
Date Of Birth 27 Jan 1983

Lee Grant joined Manchester United on 3 July 2018 by everyone's surprise, especially Manchester United fans. Lee Grant was not well known and fans were against the fact that he was signed and not a fresh young talent that everyone thought the club was looking to sign. As a result the reaction was confusion and that turned to doubt, however when he played as a starting 11 in a pre-season tournament, Lee Grant showed the fans that he was not just some old washed up football player and showed his class during that game. It is safe to say that Manchester United's goalkeeping position is safe and loaded especially with Lee Grant as the last resort.
