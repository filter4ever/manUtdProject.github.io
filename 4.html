Phil Jones

Player's Bio
Position Centre Back
Country England
Date Of Birth 12 Dec 1994

Ahh... the meme himself. Phil Jones joined the club on 1 July 2011. Despite being one of the most despised Manchester United players, his entrance into the club was a big one. He made his debut against Manchester City in the second half replacing Rio Ferdinand when Manchester United was losing 2-0. Immediately having big shoes to fill in the Manchester Derby, most would feel overwhelmed and prone to giving a bad performance. However, Manchester United managed to comeback to a 2-3 win. He made his league debut a week later again as a substitution for Rio Ferdinand against West Bromwich Albion in a 2-1 win. On 22 August 2011, he made his starting 11 debut against Tottenham Hotspur in a 3-0 win at home. So his first impression in his first season was a very good one and the future looked very promising. However, as we know by now, he did not live up to what was expected of him, whether it was injuries or lack of playing time, we as fans can only dream of when Phil Jones finally be a world class Manchester United has been looking for.
