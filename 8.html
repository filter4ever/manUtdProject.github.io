Juan Mata

Player's Bio
Position Midfielder
Country Spain
Date Of Birth 28 Apr 1988

The Maestro of the team. Despite his age, he just keeps demonstrating while form is temporary, class is permanent. His sublime dribbling and flawless passing that only a spanish player could replicate is a sight to behold for the fans and is appreciated by everyone. Being one of the most loved players in the football world, he donates 1% of his weekly salary to help various charaties. Showing that he has class both on and off the pitch. The club is definitely appreeciative that a player as humble as him is also representing the club and vice versa. In terms of performance, although he lost some of his agility and speed he used to possess, he is still a fan favourite within the club.
