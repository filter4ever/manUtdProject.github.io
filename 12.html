Chris Smalling

Player's Bio
Position Centre Back
Country England
Date Of Birth 22 Nov 1989

Chris Smalling joined the club on 7 July 2010. A towering 194cm in height, he has a dominant presence at the back and it is hard for oppositions to ignore. Crosses from the wings would be dealt with by Smalling easily. Not only does he stay at the back, he is no stranger to scoring important goals for the club. For example, on 8 July 2011 he scored a volley goal against none other than Manchester City to reduce the deficit to 2-1 and Manchester United went on to win the game 3-2 that day. Now one of the main centre backs for the club, he has been expected to give a more consistent performance, however fans feel like he still has some things to improve on defensively.
